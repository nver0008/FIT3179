2021-22 Football Season Transfer Review

Reviewing last year's transfer windows!

Most expensive transfers 2021:

JACK GREALISH

Signed from Aston Villa for a world record fee for an Englishmen, Jack Grealish is now playing for Manchester City as a creative attacking midfielder. His dazzling feet make him difficult to stop and is the most fouled player in the division.

  • AGE: 27
  • NATIONALITY: England
  • CLUB: Manchester City
  • LEAGUE: Premier League
  • FEE: €117,500,000

Romelu Lukaku

Signed from Inter Milan, the striker helped the Italian outfit to their first title since 2010. The Belgian returns to Chelsea for a club-record transfer fee, after previously leaving the South London club for Everton in 2014. He also had a lergely failed spell at Manchester United.

  • AGE: 29
  • NATIONALITY: Belgium
  • CLUB: Chelsea FC
  • LEAGUE: Premier League
  • FEE: €115,000,000

Jadon Sancho

Signed from Borussia Dortmund, Jadon Sancho began his career at famous Mancunian rivals Manchester City. He then made his move to Germany, where he played at Borussia Dortmund for 4 years. The versatile winger has 23 caps for the English National Team.

  • AGE: 22
  • NATIONALITY: England
  • CLUB: Manchester United
  • LEAGUE: Premier League
  • FEE: €85,000,000

Achraf Hakimi

Achraf Hakimi is a Moroccon international footballer that was also signed from Inter Milan in Italy. He started his career at Real Madrid back in 2016, before a 2-year loan spell at German outfit Borussia Dortmund. He is currently the world's most expensive right back.

  • AGE: 23
  • NATIONALITY: Morocco
  • CLUB: Paris Saint Germain
  • LEAGUE: Ligue 1
  • FEE: €60,000,000

Clubs with Highest Gross Spend in 2021:

Arsenal FC

Club based in North London, UK, the Gunners finished the 2021-22 season in 5th place, 2 points behind noisy neighbours Tottenham Hotspur.

  • LEAGUE: Premier League
  • FEES: €165,600,000
  • MOST EXPENSIVE PLAYER: Benjamin White

RB Leipzig

Club based in Liepzig, Germany, was founded in 2009 by Red Bull who had the intent of building the club up from the 5th division to the top flight.

  • LEAGUE: Bundesliga
  • FEES: €107,620,000
  • MOST EXPENSIVE PLAYER: André Silva

Atletico Madrid

Club based in Madrid, Spain, Atleti employ argentine head coach Diego Simeone, known for his stout defensive record. They won La Liga during the 2020-21 season.

  • FEES: €71,000,000
  • MOST EXPENSIVE PLAYER: Rodrigo De Paul

Paris SG

Commonly known as PSG, they are based in Paris, France, and were founded in the year 1970. They have won 8 out of the last 10 division titles.

  • LEAGUE: Ligue 1
  • FEES: €83,000,000
  • MOST EXPENSIVE PLAYER: Achraf Hakimi

AS Roma

Club based in Rome, Italy, Roma were founded in 1927. Now with famous manager José Mourinho at the helm, they won the inaugural UEFA Conference League.
